Exposure Logger Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

Overview

Exposure Logger is an offline-first mobile app for film photographers. The app stores your rolls, exposures, gear entries, and app settings locally on your device.

Data the app can access

  • Microphone / speech recognition: only when you explicitly start voice input.
  • Location: only when you enable or request location capture.
  • Files: only when you export or import backups and exports.

How data is used

  • Roll, exposure, gear, and settings data are used to provide the app's logging features.
  • Voice input is used to transcribe and parse exposure metadata that you choose to record.
  • Location data is used to attach optional coordinates to exposures.
  • Export and backup files are created only when you explicitly request them.

Storage and sharing

  • The app does not require an account.
  • The app is designed to keep your data local to your device.
  • Data is only shared outside the app when you explicitly export or share it.
  • No data is sold.

Third-party services

Exposure Logger relies on platform services provided by Android, iOS, Expo, and React Native components to access permissions such as speech recognition and location. Those platform services may process data as required to provide their functionality.

Your control

  • You can choose whether to use voice input.
  • You can choose whether to use location capture.
  • You can export or import your data using the in-app backup features.
  • You can delete the app and its local data from your device at any time.
